Who Inspires Me

The Legends Behind the Gallery

♚

Magnus Carlsen

The reigning era's undisputed king. Magnus showed me that chess isn't just about memorising openings — it's about understanding positions deeply and outplaying your opponent in any phase of the game.

Grandmaster
♔

Bobby Fischer

Possibly the most gifted chess mind in history. Fischer's obsessive dedication and refusal to settle for anything less than perfection is something I carry into every training session.

Grandmaster
♞

Levy Rozman (GothamChess)

The content creator who made chess click for me. Levy's breakdowns of famous games and his ability to explain complex ideas simply inspired me to take my own game seriously.

International Master
⚡

Hikaru Nakamura

Pure speed and creativity. Hikaru's bullet chess is almost supernatural — watching him find solutions in seconds while I'm still thinking in minutes is both humbling and motivating.

Grandmaster
♛

Beth Harmon (The Queen's Gambit)

Fictional — but the story hit real. Her journey from nothing to the world stage, driven by pure love for the game, mirrors what I want my own chess story to look like one day.
