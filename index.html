My Story

From Struggle
to Strategy

"Every setback is just a move that sets up a comeback."

I'm Miguel — an 18-year-old BIDA student at Botswana Accountancy College, driven by purpose and fuelled by a past I refuse to repeat.

Like many students, my path wasn't always straight. Moving from place to place during my junior years disrupted my mindset, and the freedom I found hit me at the wrong time — I barely passed my JCE with a C+. That result haunts me to this day, and the disappointment I saw on my mother's face became the fire I carry everywhere.

But I listened. I locked in. By the time Senior school was done, I had pulled off 45 points in my BGCSE — a score that opened the door to exactly what I wanted: a course specialising in data management and IT. With the world accelerating into an AI-driven era, I know that the IT department is where opportunities will explode. I'm positioning myself right in the middle of it.
